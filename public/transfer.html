Call for advice or discuss transfer

Transport and retrieval services (NETS, NSW Ambulance), LHD virtual care and Paediatric Major Trauma Centres are available to:

  • Provide time-critical virtual support while you manage the injured child
  • Assist with on-the-ground decision making about management and transfer
  • Plan for transfer

Not every patient needs to be transferred — these services are advice lines as well.

GOAL: Call early for advice and support

  • NETS – Clinical Hub – Home (1300 362 500) to access the virtual paediatric trauma team, assistance with resuscitation and early management, and to discuss transfer options
  • For Group 1 children with high-grade injuries; or Group 2 and 3 children; or where other significant concerns exist
  • PATCH: Paediatric Acute Trauma Care Hotline (SCHN) (flowchart)
  • or LHD Virtual Care (details)
  • For Group 1 children with minimal signs but a concerning mechanism
