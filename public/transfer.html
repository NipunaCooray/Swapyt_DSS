Call for advice or discuss transfer

Goal: GOAL: Call early for advice and support

  • Obtain time critical virtual advice and support while you manage the injured child
  • Receive assistance with on the ground decision making about management best place of care for the child
  • Coordinate retrieval if required

Not every patient needs to be transferred — these services are advice lines as well.

Call NETS 1300 362 500 LINK NETS - Clinical Hub - Home to access the virtual paediatric trauma team, obtain assistance with resuscitation and early management, and to discuss transfer options
